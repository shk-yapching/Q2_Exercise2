Momentum

  • Describes an objects "quantitiy of motion"
  • p=mv
  • SI unit: Kg·m/s
  • A vector quantity
  • Its direction is the same as the velocity (v)

Impulse

  • The product of the net force acting on an object and the time interval during which the force is applied.
  • Ft=Δmv
  • SI unit: N·s
  • A vector quantity
  • Its direction is the same as the net force (F)

The Mole

  • The quantity used by the chemist to express the enormouse number of tiny particles in each sample of matter.
  • One mole of a substance is the amount of substance that contains the same number of particles as the number of atoms in exactly 12 grams of Carbon-12 isotope.
  • Defines a relationship between the atomic mass unit and the gram.
  • =The mas in grams of one mole of a substance is numerically equal to its molar mass.
